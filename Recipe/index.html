Bengali Rich Food

Paratha

Paratha is the daily breakfast of bengali people. It served with mixed vegetables, dal, egg, and milk-tea.

Morog-Polao

Morog-Polao is a traditional bengali food. It is made with chicken, rice, and various spices. It is often served on special occasions.

Mutton-Biryani

Mutton-Biryani is a popular bengali food. It is made with mutton, rice, and various spices. It is often served on special occasions.

Sorisha-Beef-Tehari

Sorisha-Beef-Tehari is a traditional bengali food. It is made with beef, rice, and mustard oil. It is often served on special occasions.

Khicuri

Khicuri is a traditional bengali food. It is made with rice, lentils, and various spices. It is often served on rainy days. People mostly enjoy it on rainy day.

Beef-Chap-Polao

Beef-Chap-Polao is a traditional bengali food. It is made with beef, rice, and various spices. It is often served on special occasions.
